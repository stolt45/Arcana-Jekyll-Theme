Sharon Stolt

Principal Consultant

Sharon has been designing and delivering results-oriented development programs since 2002. For nearly 15 years she has led workshops on a wide variety of leadership and professional skills, including time management, Crucial Conversations®, creativity and innovation, career development, coaching and feedback, and intercultural communication.

Part educator, part motivational speaker, Sharon creates and facilitates learning experiences that teach new knowledge and skills and inspire the behavior change needed for success.

Before she founded Stolt Consulting in 2016, Sharon was Director of Learning & Development at Visa, Inc., where she developed and launched the company’s first comprehensive manager training program, The Visa Manager Experience. In 2013, Sharon was awarded Visa’s highest honor, the annual Go Beyond Award, as a result of her work on the program. From 2015-2016, Sharon led the design and launch of the inaugural Visa University campus in Foster City, CA.

Sharon lives in Berkeley with her husband, Chris, their dog, Bailey, and their extensive collection of California wines. Sharon enjoys experimenting with new recipes and exploring nearby hiking trails, and has been conducting rigorous taste-testing for the best cupcake in the Bay Area for nearly 6 years. Sharon is passionate about causes that increase educational opportunities for all. She volunteers weekly in a special education classroom at King Child Development Center through the Berkeley Schools Fund.
